# Squamish 50 Mile - 14 Hour Pacing Workout for Garmin Fenix 6 Pro

## Multiple Ways to Get This Workout on Your Device

### Method 1: Upload TCX File (Recommended)
1. Go to [Garmin Connect Web](https://connect.garmin.com)
2. Sign in to your account
3. Click the '+' button and select 'Import Data'
4. Upload the `squamish50mile_workout.tcx` file
5. The workout will appear in Training > Workouts
6. Send to your Fenix 6 Pro via Garmin Connect app

### Method 3: Direct File Transfer (Advanced)
1. Connect your Fenix 6 Pro to computer via USB
2. Copy the .fit file (when available) to `/GARMIN/NEWFILES/`
3. Safely eject the device
4. The workout will appear in your device's workout menu

### Using the Workout on Your Fenix 6 Pro
1. On your watch, go to: Menu > Training > Workouts
2. Select 'Squamish 50 Mile 14h Pacing'
3. Press START to begin the workout
4. The watch will guide you through each segment with pace targets
5. You'll get alerts when transitioning between segments

### Tips for Race Day
- Start the workout when you begin the race
- The pace targets are guidelines - adjust based on conditions
- Use the lap button to manually advance segments if needed
- Monitor your heart rate and perceived effort
- Save and upload your activity after the race
