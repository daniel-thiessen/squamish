# Squamish 50 Mile – 14 Hour Pacing Plan (with early fatigue factored)

## AS0 → AS1 (0 – 10 km)
- **Terrain:** Easy start on road & doubletrack, then rolling singletrack
- **Pace:** 7:00–7:40/km
- **Estimated Time:** 1:14
- **Notes:** Hold back, keep HR low, avoid burning quads on early rollers.

## AS1 → AS2 (10 – 24 km)
- **Terrain:** Steep climb (Crumpit Woods), technical descent, long Galactic Gravity climb
- **Pace:** Climbs 10:00–11:30/km, descents 6:40–7:10/km
- **Estimated Time:** 2:20
- **Notes:** Early fatigue prevention—power hike climbs, steady cadence.

## AS2 → AS3 (24 – 36 km)
- **Terrain:** Flowy descent, then major climb
- **Pace:** Descents 6:50–7:10/km, climb 11:00–12:20/km
- **Estimated Time:** 2:03
- **Notes:** Eat early, hydrate—this is where fatigue can start.

## AS3 → AS4 (36 – 51 km)
- **Terrain:** Mixed descent to Alice Lake, steep climb to Corners, flowy descent
- **Pace:** Descents 7:20–7:50/km, climbs 11:30–12:40/km
- **Estimated Time:** 2:11
- **Notes:** Keep effort capped—this section can drain legs.

## AS4 → AS5 (51 – 64 km)
- **Terrain:** Rolling climb over Debeck’s Hill, steep descent
- **Pace:** Climb 12:00–13:20/km, descent 7:40–8:10/km
- **Estimated Time:** 2:12
- **Notes:** Manage nutrition closely, use descents to reset heart rate.

## AS5 → Finish (64 – 80.5 km)
- **Terrain:** Final big climb, then long descent
- **Pace:** Climb 12:40–14:00/km, descent 8:00–8:30/km
- **Estimated Time:** 2:44
- **Notes:** Expect heavy fatigue, keep moving, run downhill smoothly to avoid cramping.
